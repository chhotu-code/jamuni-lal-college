About Us

Jamuni Lal College, commonly known as JL College, is a college in Hajipur in the Indian state of Bihar. It is a constituent college of Babasaheb Bhimrao Ambedkar Bihar University, Muzaffarpur, Bihar, India.[1] This government college [2] is in Hajipur, Vaishali District, Bihar, India.
